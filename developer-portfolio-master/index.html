    This Congregation is the body of Christ and Communion of Saints. All power in this Church belongs to our Lord Jesus Christ, its head.

  • Our Mission

    This Congregation exists and strives to fulfill the Great Commission of Our Lord Jesus Christ as it is written in the Gospel of Matthew 28:18-20, Mark 16: 15-20; and to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ both locally and abroad. a. This congregation is called to serve mainly the lost souls and reach out to all people with message of Good News.

STATEMENT OF FAITH

Confession

This Congregation believes, confesses, and teaches the Triune God, the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Salvation

This Congregation believes, teaches, and confesses that Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior,and salvation is by grace through faith alone.

Holy Scripture

This Congregation believes that the Holy Bible is the Word of God, and believes, teaches, and accepts the canonical Scriptures of the Old and New Testament is inspired Word of God and the sole authoritative source and norm of our proclamation, faith, and life.

MINISTRIES

Women's Ministry

Fosters prayer and Bible study of the women of this Church. Plans meetings and retreats Plans visitation and support of needy women of this church.

Prayer Ministry

Prayer ministry is the backbone for ministries of this church. Plans and implements for monthly fasting prayer of this church.

Children and Youth ministry

Designs curriculum for kids Sunday School Plans for kids’ summer camps and retreat. Educates kids with relevant Biblical and theological teachings to tackle the contemporary challenges.

Worship Team

Services on Sunday regular services, conferences, and other church services.

Young Adult

Fosters fellowship and prayer programs for Young adults group of this Church Plans for premarital and post marital Biblical teachings of Marriage with the Pastoral office to overcome the current challenges of the family in our context. Focus on raising future leaders for the church together with the Pastoral office.

Deacons Ministry

Wholistic Ministry

Serves the motto of this Church that serving the whole person is its priority. Works on ways to raise funds for most needy people to reach out with possible support.
